Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

1. Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your information.

2. Information We Collect

  • OAuth access tokens for TikTok and YouTube (used only for video uploads)
  • Email addresses if you sign up using email/password

3. How We Use Information

  • To upload videos on your behalf to TikTok and YouTube
  • To provide account and service functionality

4. Data Storage

Tokens and user data are encrypted and stored securely. We do not store passwords.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except as required by law.

6. User Rights

You can revoke access or delete your account at any time. Revoking OAuth tokens disables uploads.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of changes.
